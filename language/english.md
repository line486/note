### 英语语法综述

**简单句**：不能再拆开的基本句称为“简单句”。

**结构**：什么 + 怎么样

> [!NOTE]
>
> **什么**：主语，人/物；
>
> **怎么样**：谓语，动作/发生了什么事。

**动作(动词)**：

| 类型                          | 说明                 | 谓语动词     | 基本句型                                  | 示例                                                  |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| 可以独立完成的动作            | 无需承受者（宾 语）  | 不及物物动词 | 主语 + 不及物物动词                       | Papa Rabbit sleeps. _兔老爹睡觉。_                    |
| 有1个动作的承受者             | 一个宾语，无补充信息 | 单及物动词   | 主语 + 单及物动词 + 宾语                  | Papa Rabbit likes you. _兔老爹喜欢你。_               |
| 有2个动作承受者               | 两个宾语             | 双及物动词   | 主语 + 双及物动词 + 间接宾语 + 直接宾语   | Papa Rabbit teaches you English. _兔老爹教你英语。_   |
| 只有1个动作承受者（但需补充） | 一个宾语 + 补充信息  | 复杂及物动词 | 主语 + 复杂及物动词 + 宾语 + （宾语）补语 | Papa Rabbit considers you smart. _兔老爹认为你聪明。_ |
| 非“动作”                      | 赋 予 信 息          | 系动词       | 主语 + 系动词 + （主语）补语/表语         | Papa Rabbit smells nice. _兔老爹闻上去香。_           |

> [!NOTE]
>
> 严格说来，谓语和谓语动词有区别。句子主语后面的余下句子部分就是谓语，谓语动词只是谓语的一部分。通常习惯上，把谓语动词直接称为谓语。

**句子成分**

主语、谓语动词、宾语、宾语补语、主语补语上文（已介绍），

定语：The little white **rabbit** ate a large **carrot**. _这只小白兔吃了一根大胡萝卜。_

状语：The rabbit ate **quickly**. _兔子快速地吃。_

同位语：**Papa Rabbit**, **an English teacher**, eats carrots. _兔老爹，一个英语老师，吃胡萝卜。_

**简单句组合**：将简单句进行组合，可得到复合句（主从句）和并列句。

> [!NOTE]
>
> 从句：把简单句修改一下来充当主句的句子成分。

| 类型           | 说明       | 备注       |
| -------------- | ---------- | ---------- |
| 简单句作主语   | 主语从句   | 名词性从句 |
| 简单句作宾语   | 宾 语从句  | 名词性从句 |
| 简单句作表语   | 表 语从句  | 名词性从句 |
| 简单句作同位语 | 同位语从句 | 名词性从句 |
| 简单句作定语   | 定语从句   |            |
| 简单句作状语   | 状语从句   |            |

**词性**：

| 词性   | 示例                                                                                                   |
| ------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 名词   | Papa Rabbit is a **rabbit**. _兔老爹是只兔子。_                                                        |
| 冠词   | Papa Rabbit is **a** rabbit. _兔老爹是只兔子。_                                                        |
| 代词   | **I** am a rabbit. _我是只兔子。_                                                                      |
| 形容词 | I am a **smart** rabbit. _我是只聪明的兔子。_                                                          |
| 数词   | I ate **two** carrots. _我吃了两根胡萝卜。_                                                            |
| 副词   | I ate two carrots **quickly**. _我很快地吃了两根胡萝卜。_                                              |
| 介词   | I ate two carrots **with** chopsticks. _我用筷子吃了两根胡萝卜。_                                      |
| 叹词   | **Ah**, the carrot is tasty! *啊，胡萝卜好美味！*                                                      |
| 连词   | I ate two carrots and a potato, **because** I was hungry. *我吃了两根胡萝卜和一个马铃薯，因为我饿了。* |

> [!NOTE]
>
> 句子成分和词类没有一一对应关系，除了谓语动词是动词，其他成分可能包含不同的词类。

**谓语动词的“三大本领”**：

| 类型             | 说明                 | 备注 |
| ---------------- | -------------------- | ---- |
| 动作时间         | 过去、现在、将来     | 时态 |
| 动作状态         | 已经完成、正在进行等 | 时态 |
| 动作假设、情感等 | 语气                 |      |

> [!NOTE]
>
> 语气分为虚拟语气、陈述语气、祈使语气

**助动词**

英语的谓语动词本身往往是不能够独立完成上述三大本领，且谓语动词也无法独自完成否定、可能性、必须性等意思。助动词帮助谓语动词发挥以上本领。

不要把助动词和它其他的身份（如作实义动词时）弄混淆。

do 可以作为助动词，也可以是实义动词“做”。

have 可以作为助动词，也可以是实义动词“有”

**非谓语动词**

非谓语动词几乎可以取代所有的从句，从而简化句子。但这样，非谓语动词就不再具有表示动作时间、状态语态、语气的功能。

## 动词笔记

### 一、 五大基本句型

1. **主语无需承受者（宾语）**
    - 结构：主语 + 不及物动词
    - 例：The rabbit slept. (兔子睡了)

2. **一个宾语，无补充信息**
    - 结构：主语 + 谓语动词 + 宾语
    - 例：The rabbit ate a carrot. (兔子吃了一根胡萝卜)

3. **两个宾语**
    - 结构：主语 + 双及物动词 + 间接宾语 + 直接宾语
    - 例：The rabbit gave them a carrot. (兔子给了他们一根胡萝卜)

4. **一个宾语 + 补充信息**
    - 结构：主语 + 复杂及物动词 + 宾语 + 宾语补语
    - 例：The rabbit considered the carrot tasty. (兔子认为这胡萝卜好吃)

5. **赋予信息**
    - 结构：主语 + 系动词 + 主语补语/表语
    - 例：The rabbit is smart. (兔子很聪明)

### 二、 动词分类

#### 1. 及物动词 vs 不及物动词

- **及物动词**：动作是有对象的，即需要带宾语（承受者）。
- **不及物动词**：动作没有对象，无需承受者（宾语）。

#### 2. 实义动词（按状态/动作划分）

- **动作动词**：如 eat
- **状态动词**：如 have, own

> **注**：实义动词可作谓语动词。

#### 3. 非谓语动词（不可作谓语动词）

- 不定式
- 过去分词
- 现在分词
- 动名词

### 三、 系动词（连系动词）

**核心功能**：赋值。将后面的形容词或名词赋予给主语。

- 例1：I am a rabbit. / The rabbit is smart.（将 smart 赋予给 rabbit）
- 例2：The carrot smells nice.（将 nice 赋予给 carrot）
- 例3：The room remained empty.（房间一直是空的）
- 例4：The sky grew dark.

**常见系动词**：
look, feel, stay, become, seem, turn, appear, grow 等。

### 四、 助动词

**定义**：谓语动词本身往往无法独立表达某些语法概念，需要其他词的辅助。这些辅助构成谓语动词，但自己本身不能做谓语动词的词，就是助动词。

**助动词的两个基本特征**：

1. 辅助主动词
2. 不能像主动词那样单独作谓语动词

#### （一） 基本助动词

1. **be**（存在）：构成进行时。
    - 例：The rabbit is eating a carrot.
2. **do**（做）：构成疑问、否定或强调（还可作实义动词）。
    - 例：The rabbit does like carrots. (构成强调)
3. **have**（有）：构成完成时。
    - 例：The rabbit has eaten a carrot.

#### （二） 情态助动词（情态动词）

**情态动词的两个基本特征**：

1. 起辅助主动词的作用
2. 不能单独作谓语动词

> 因此，情态动词也是助动词的一种，也可称"情态助动词"。

##### 1. can / could

- **有能力做/会/可以**：I can kill a wolf. (表示有能力，但不一定会做)
- **表请求或许可**：Can I borrow your book? Yes, of course. (could 比 can 更诚恳些)
- **表可能性**：Anything can happen. / Anything could happen. (can 表可能，could 表对过去的推测和现在的可能)

##### 2. may / might

- **表请求或许可**：May/Might I borrow your book? Yes, of course. (might 比 may 更礼貌)
- **表可能性**（可能性程度小于 can/could）：The rabbit isn't there. He may be at home.
- **表祝愿**：May you succeed! (祝你成功)

##### 3. must

- **表必需**：You must finish your work.
- **表禁止**：You must not / mustn't smoke. (禁止吸烟。must not 不是"不必须"，而是"必须不！")
- **表推测**：The light is on. The rabbit must be at home.
    - **否定推测**：The light isn't on. The rabbit can't be at home. (否定推测用 can't)

##### 4. will / would

- **构成将来时**：The rabbit will eat a carrot. / The rabbit would eat a carrot.
- **表示请求、建议**：Will you lend me that book? (是否愿意) / Would you please...?
- **表示推测、假设**：Ask him. He would know. (他可能知道)

##### 5. shall

- **构成将来时**（同 will 用法）：I shall be there at 9 AM.
- **表示请求或征求意见**：Shall we meet at 9 AM?
- **表警告、命令**：You shall not steal!

##### 6. should

- **表应当**
- **表有一定推测**：It should rain tomorrow.
- **表竟然**：It's surprising that he should be late. (体现惊讶、愤怒或失望等)
- **构成虚拟语气**

##### 7. ought to

- 表应该，与 should 用法大致相同，但语气更强一些。
- 例：We ought to treat the rabbit well.

##### 8. need

- **实义动词**：需要
- **情态动词**：有必要（否定时在 need 后加 not）
    - 例：The rabbit need not wait.

##### 9. dare

- **实义动词**：敢，挑战，冒险
- **情态动词**：敢
    - 例：The rabbit dare not tell the truth to the wolf.

##### 10. used to

- 表过去的动作，尤其是重复的动作，**强调和现在的对比**。
- 例：The rabbit used to eat apples. Now he only eats carrots.
- **区别**：be used to 表示习惯于。

### 五、 半助动词

1. **be able to**
    - 可以表达将来的能力：I'll be able to reach school on time if I leave right now.
    - was/were able to：成功做成某事。

2. **be going to**
    - 有更强的主观能动性，计划做某事。
    - 例：I'm going to eat dinner at 6 PM today.

3. **be about to**
    - 马上就要做。

4. **be to**
    - 被命令做某事：You are to finish homework first.
    - 命中注定：I think they are to fall in love with each other.

5. **had better** (缩写 'd better)
    - 最好做，有忠告、警告之意。
    - 例：You had better follow my order.

### 六、 使役动词

**定义**：需直接接宾语以及宾语补语的动词有好几类，其中表达"使、让、令"的一类动词称为使役动词。

**最常见的四个使役动词及宾语补语情况**：

| 使役动词 | 宾语补语形式        | 例句                                                                                         |
| :------- | :------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **make** | 动词原形 (裸不定式) | I make the wolf eat carrots from time to time.                                               |
|          | 形容词              | The carrot made the wolf full.                                                               |
| **let**  | 动词原形 (裸不定式) | Let me go!                                                                                   |
|          | 副词                | Let him out.                                                                                 |
| **have** | 动词原形 (裸不定式) | I'll have the wolf wash the dishes. (给某人任务/责任做)                                      |
|          | 过去分词            | I have my car washed. (让某事被做) <br> I had my carrot stolen yesterday. (也可能是遭遇不幸) |
| **get**  | to 不定式           | I get the wolf to clean the kitchen after she washes dishes. (说服、鼓励做...)               |
|          | 过去分词            | I got my nail done.                                                                          |

### 七、 情态动词表推测（可能性大小）

从极其确定到极其不确定的可能性顺序为：
**must > will > would > ought to > should > can > could > may > might**

> **注**：所有过去式的可能性都比原形的可能性稍微降低一些。
> 去除过去式，即：**must > will > ought to > should > can > may**

**举例说明（可能性递减）**：

- **确定**：It will rain tomorrow. (明天会下雨，很确定)
- **可能性较大**：You would give this video a thumb-up.
- **可能**：It can rain tomorrow. (不确定，仅可能)
- **可能性减少**：You could give me a thumb.
- **很不确定**：It may rain tomorrow.
- **可能性极小**：You might forward this video to a friend.

## 非谓语动词与虚拟语气

### 一、 非谓语动词概述

**谓语动词（限定性动词）**：受主语限制，被限定。

**非谓语动词（非限定性动词）**：不受主语限制，不被限定的动词。

例：
I like to eat carrots. / I liked eating carrots.
I'm a talking rabbit. / The rabbit likes minced carrots.

一个动词，作为谓语动词只能在句中充当核心的谓语动词，而不能充当其他句子成分。如果想充当其他句子成分（包括：主语、宾语、宾语补足语、主语补足语/表语、定语、状语），必须转为非谓语动词，即：不定式、过去分词、现在分词、动名词其中的一个。

### 二、 动词不定式

**形式**：to + 动词原形（如：To be or not to be, that is the question.）

有时 "to" 也可以省略（如：You helped me (to) cook the carrot.）

#### 1. 不定式作主语

例：To eat a carrot every day is good for the rabbit.

不定式充当主语，只看 "To eat" 不知道是谁吃，不知是过去吃还是将来吃，所以是不定式。

常用句型（用 it 作形式主语）：It is good for the rabbit to eat a carrot every day.

类似主语从句：It is good that the rabbit eats a carrot every day.

#### 2. 不定式作宾语

例：The rabbit likes to eat carrots. (谓语like，宾语to eat)

复合宾语结构：I consider it important to eat a carrot every day. (it为形式宾语，不定式为真正宾语)

不定式与复杂及物动词连用：The rabbit expected the wolf to eat a carrot. (兔子希望狼发出的动作)

感官动词与使役动词的省略：see, find, watch, have, make, let 后面用不定式作宾补时，需省略 "to"。

#### 3. 不定式作主语补足语（表语）

例：The rabbit's dream is to eat every kind of carrot in the world.

相当于：To eat every kind of carrot in the world is the rabbit's dream. (不定式作主语)

#### 4. 不定式作定语

例：The rabbit has a lot of carrots to eat.

后置定语，修饰 carrots，表示这些胡萝卜是会被吃的。

#### 5. 不定式作状语（表达原因、目的和结果）

表原因：I was surprised to get a thumbs-up. = I was surprised because I got a thumbs-up. (原因状语从句)

表目的：I will do anything to get a thumbs-up. = I will do anything in order that I get a thumbs-up. (目的状语从句)

表结果：I got enough thumbs-up to make another video. = I got enough thumbs-up, so that I made another video. (结果状语从句)

#### 高级不定式（把不定式和状态相结合）

- to be eating a carrot：to + 进行状态
    - The rabbit seemed to be eating a carrot.
- to have eaten a carrot：to + 完成状态
- to have been eating a carrot：to + 完成进行状态
    - 这兔子之前似乎在吃一根胡萝卜（的过程中）

### 三、 动名词

**本质**：相当于名词。**形式**：动词原形 + ing

#### 1. 动名词作主语

例：Eating carrots is healthy for the rabbit. (主语 + 系动词)

#### 2. 动名词作主语补语（表语）

例：The rabbit's hobby is growing carrots. / Seeing is believing. (眼见为实)

#### 3. 动名词作定语

例：a sleeping pill → a pill for sleeping

#### 4. 动名词作宾语

例：The rabbit likes eating carrots.

只能接动名词作宾语的动词/短语：enjoy, avoid, advise, consider, escape, mind, practice, insist on, give up, feel like, put off

如：The rabbit enjoys eating carrots.

介词 + 宾语（动名词）：
The rabbit is fond of eating carrots.
The rabbit is looking forward to seeing the wolf again. (to为介词)

#### 不定式作宾语与动名词作宾语的区别

**stop**：
The rabbit stopped to eat a carrot. (停下手中的活去吃一根)
The rabbit stopped eating a carrot. (停止吃一根)

**forget**：
forget + to do (忘记去做，未做)

### 四、 过去分词

**常见结构**：动词原形 + -ed，但也有很多不规则变化（如：eat→eaten, see→seen, cut→cut, grow→grown, tell→told）。

> **注意**：过去分词不自带时间，不能表示过去时间。

#### 过去分词作定语

相当于形容词，单独一个过去分词作形容词前置：minced carrot (绞碎的胡萝卜), a painted room, an excited rabbit.

过去分词短语作定语，则要放在被修饰名词之后（后置定语）：
The carrot cake eaten by the rabbit is delicious.

#### 1. 过去分词作主语补足语（表语）

包含被动关系，被描述的是动作的接受者：
The rabbit is interested in growing carrots. (对...感兴趣的)

#### 2. 过去分词作宾语补足语

例：The rabbit found his carrot stolen. (宾语carrot的补语)

#### 3. 过去分词作状语

表时间/地点/条件：Seen from the hill, the carrot field looks beautiful. (从山上看，胡萝卜田看上去很美。carrot field 是 Seen 的逻辑主语，被动关系，即被看)

表原因：Born in a rabbit family, the rabbit's only work is growing carrots. (由于出生在兔子家庭)

表条件：Given another chance, the rabbit would go to the wolf's party.

**现在分词与过去分词的区别**：在于主语与动作之间的主动或被动关系。主动用现在分词，被动用过去分词。

#### 独立主格

The work finished, the rabbit went home. (工作完成后，兔子回家了。名词/代词 + 分词，名词与分词构成逻辑主谓/被动关系，且不受主句主语限制)

The weather permitting, the rabbit will go out. (天气允许的话，兔子会出门。主动语态)

### 五、 虚拟语气 (Subjunctive Mood)

**核心原则**：时态后退原则

#### 一、 表不可能假设

描述脑中的想象，或与现实相反的、主观的意愿。

##### 1. 和现在事实相反

条件句：过去式（如果是be，则用were）
主句：would + 动词原形

例：
If I knew it, I would tell you. (如果我现在知道，那么我会告诉你) → 并非过去时间
If I saw the rabbit now, I would bite him.
If I were a boy...

##### 2. 和将来事实相反

真的不可能，或可能性很小。

条件句：should + 动词原形
主句：would + 动词原形

例：
If I should see the rabbit tomorrow, I would bite him.
If we should meet after long years, how should I greet thee? (万一我们将来重逢，我又该如何面对你呢？可能性极小)

##### 3. 和过去事实相反

假设对过去某一点有影响，用完成态。

条件句：had + 动词过去分词
主句：would have + 动词过去分词

例：
If I had watched this video before, I would have aced my test.
If I had seen the rabbit a few days ago, I would have bitten him.

#### 二、 表达愿望、请求、建议、命令等

希望某动作/事件发生，但可能性小。

##### 1. wish 的用法

对现在的希望：wish + 动词过去式
I wish (that) I were a rabbit.
I wish you were here.

对过去的希望：wish + had / would have + 动词过去分词
I wish (that) I had eaten the carrot. (对过去愿望，吃过了已经)

对将来的希望：wish + should / would / could / might + 动词原形
I wish I could eat the carrot.

##### 2. would rather 的用法

对现在的希望：I would rather you didn't eat the carrot. (假设没吃)

对过去的希望：I would rather that you hadn't eaten the carrot.

##### 3. 建议/命令类虚拟

demand, suggest, insist, order... + (should) + 动词原形

##### 4. It is (high) time that...

It is (high) time that you ate a carrot. [定语从句虚拟语气]

## 时态与不规则动词规律

### 一、 16种时态 (时间 + 状态)

#### 1. 一般现在时

基本结构：S + V ; S + V-s / es

基本信息：

- 表达事实
- 表达习惯、重复的动作
- 表达预计发生的事（如：The bus leaves at 8 o'clock tonight.）

#### 2. 现在进行时

基本结构：be动词 + V-ing (现在分词)

基本信息：

- 表达某事在说话时正在进行。
- 表达某些事在将来要发生。（如：We are going to Paris next week.）

#### 3. 现在完成时

基本结构：have / has + 过去分词 (regular verb + -ed)

基本信息：表达一件事情已经完成。

#### 4. 现在完成进行时

基本结构：have / has + been + V-ing (现在分词)

基本信息：表达一件事在某一时间内发生了但还未结束。

#### 5. 一般过去时

基本结构：S + V-ed (过去式)

基本信息：表达一件事在过去某时刻已完成。

#### 6. 过去进行时

基本结构：was / were + V-ing

基本信息：表达在过去某一时间点，该动作正在进行。

> **评析：现在完成时 VS 一般过去时 VS 过去进行时**
>
> 现在完成时：对现在有影响（如：I have gone to Shanghai. 去未回）
>
> 一般过去时：在过去的某时间里做了（如：I went to Shanghai. 去了已回）
>
> 过去进行时：在过去某时间里进行中

#### 7. 过去完成时 [过去的过去]

基本结构：had + 过去分词

基本信息：表达过去某一点的过去对该点的影响，用英语表述为 One event happened before another in the past, and have influence to another.

例：He was tired because he hadn't slept well.

#### 8. 过去完成进行时

基本结构：had + been + V-ing

基本信息：对于过去某个点，不但已经完成一部分，而且在过去那一点之后，还要继续一段时间。

#### 9. 一般将来时

基本结构：S + will + V (原形) ; S + be going to + V (原形)

基本信息：表达将来某个时间点发生的动作（说话者十分确定的事）。

#### 10. 将来进行时

基本结构：will + be + V-ing

基本信息：表达在将来某个时间，某个动作是在进行的过程中。（特别笃定某事一定发生）

#### 11. 将来完成时

基本结构：will + have + 过去分词

基本信息：表达将来某一点的过去对将来那一点的影响（特别特别确信的事）。

例：The rabbit will have eaten a carrot (tomorrow).

#### 12. 将来完成进行时 (很少用到)

基本结构：will + have + been + V-ing

基本信息：表达对于将来某个点，不但已经完成了一部分，而且还要继续进行的动作。

例：You will have been watching my video. (将来某一时间点看了正要继续看一段时间)

#### 4种过去将来时态

相对于过去某一时间的将来。过去将来时态常出现于从句。

#### 13. 一般过去将来时

基本结构：would + V (would为助动词过去式) ; was / were going to

基本信息：表示过去的将来，表示更礼貌。也可用于虚拟语气。

例：I said I would eat a carrot for lunch. = I said I was going to eat a carrot for lunch. / I knew you would watch my video. = I knew you were going to watch my video.

#### 14. 过去将来进行时

基本信息：表达从过去某时刻看，将来某时正在进行的过程。

例：我之前说我明天/昨天下午1点会是在吃胡萝卜这个过程中。

#### 15. 过去将来完成时

基本结构：would + have + 过去分词

基本信息：表达从过去某时刻看，将来某时已经完成的动作。

例：I said I would have eaten a carrot by 1PM tomorrow/yesterday. (我之前说我明天/昨天下午1点会已经做完了吃胡萝卜这个动作。)

#### 16. 过去将来完成进行时

基本结构：would + have + been + V-ing

基本信息：表达从过去某时刻看，将来某时之前已持续了一段时间且还将继续的动作。

例：I said I would have been eating lunch for 2 hours by 1PM tomorrow/yesterday. (我之前说我明天/昨天下午1点前会已经吃了俩钟头中饭，且之后还会继续吃。)

### 二、 英语"不规则"动词"规律"

1. 过去式 = 过去分词 = 原形 (A-A-A)
2. 过去式改字母，过去分词 = 原形 (A-B-A)
3. 过去式改字母，过去分词 = 原形 + en
4. 过去式改字母，过去分词 = 过去式 + en
5. 原形 ow ➔ 过去式 ew ➔ 过去分词 own
6. 原形 i ➔ 过去式 a ➔ 过去分词 u
7. 原形 ➔ 过去式 ought ➔ 过去分词 ought
8. 原形 ➔ 过去式 aught ➔ 过去分词 aught
9. 过去式改元音字母，过去分词 = 过去式 (A-B-B)
10. 过去式改辅音字母，过去分词 = 过去式 (A-B-B)
11. 过去式改字母 + t，过去分词 = 过去式 (A-B-B)

## 独立主格

### 一、 定义与核心逻辑

**独立主格**：逻辑主语与真正主语不一致，相对"独立"。

例句对比：

原因状语从句：Because the wolf invited him, the rabbit decided to go to the party.

独立主格：The wolf inviting him, the rabbit decided to go to the party.

解析：动作由 the wolf 发出，而不是句子的真正主语 rabbit。因此，the wolf 是 invite 的逻辑主语。

### 二、 独立主格结构对从句的简化

#### 1. 引导条件状语从句

从句：If the weather permits, the rabbit will go out. (如果天气允许，兔子就会出门。)

独立主格：The weather permitting, the rabbit will go out.

#### 2. 引导时间状语从句

从句：After the work was finished, the rabbit went home. (工作完成后，兔子回家了。)

独立主格：The work finished, the rabbit went home.

#### 3. 并列句的简化

并列句：The rabbit is lying in bed and a carrot is still held in his hand. (兔子正躺在床上，胡萝卜仍在他手中。)

独立主格：The rabbit is lying in bed, a carrot still held in his hand.

### 三、 独立主格的常见结构

### 四、 独立主格的本质：省略 being

综上，这些句子更像是：包含 be 动词的独立主格，也就是 **名词/代词 + being + ...**，然后省略了 being。

名词/代词 + being + 名词：
Many animals went to the party, some of them being rabbits and wolves. ➔ some of them rabbits and wolves.

名词/代词 + being + 形容词：
Many animals went to the party, some of them being happy. ➔ some of them happy.

名词/代词 + being + 副词：
The rabbit sat on a chair, head being down. ➔ head down.

名词/代词 + being + 介词短语：
The rabbit came in, carrot being in hand. ➔ carrot in hand.

### 五、 with 复合结构与独立主格的转换

**独立主格结构 = with + 宾语 + 宾语补足语**

#### 1. 添加 with

可以加上 with，其他部分不变：

The work finished, the rabbit went home. (工作完成后，兔子回家了。)
➔ With the work finished, the rabbit went home.

#### 2. 省略 with

有一些省略 with 后，会简化句子结构（常省去代词的所有格和冠词）：

The rabbit came in, with a carrot in his hand. (兔子进来了，手里拿着一根胡萝卜。)
➔ The rabbit came in, carrot in hand. (兔子进来了，手里拿着一根胡萝卜。)

## 从句

### 一、定语从句（形容词从句、关系从句）

**关系副词与关系代词的转化**：关系副词可转化为关系代词，即 where/why/when = at which/for which/on which。因此，关系副词 = 介词 + which。

#### 限制性与非限制性

**(1) 限定性形容词从句**

限定 carrot 的范围性质。

The rabbit ate the carrot which was on the table. (兔子吃了桌子上的那一根，暗示有很多根，限定了是桌上的那根)

**(2) 非限定性形容词从句**

可以作插入语解释说明，也可指代整句话。

The rabbit ate the carrot, which was on the table. (接着补充说明这根胡萝卜在桌上)

**非限定性形容词从句作插入语**

My head, which is big, is useful when it rains. (我有大头，下雨不愁。)

The biggest carrot, which the rabbit ate, was on the table. (最大的那根胡萝卜曾在桌上，兔子吃了它。)

### 二、名词性从句

#### 1. 主语从句

形式主语 = it

That the hungry rabbit had already eaten a carrot for dinner this evening is obvious.

把主语从句换成形式主语：

Whether the rabbit ate the carrot is obvious. ➔ It is obvious whether the rabbit ate the carrot.

Where/When/How the rabbit ate the carrot is obvious. ➔ It is obvious where/when/how the rabbit ate the carrot.

Who ate the carrot is obvious. ➔ It is obvious who ate the carrot.

What the rabbit ate is obvious. ➔ It is obvious what the rabbit ate.

**特殊主语从句（虚拟语气）**：

It is suggested/demanded/insisted/ordered/important/necessary/essential that you (should) eat a carrot.
= That you (should) eat a carrot is suggested.

#### 2. 宾语从句

saw 的直接宾语是确定信息；不确定信息作宾语从句：

I saw whether/who/what/where/when/how the rabbit ate the carrot.

**(1) that 的省略**：

除了某些介词参与的情况，从句的引导词基本都在从句的开头。宾语从句中的 that 往往可以省略：

I know (that) the rabbit ate a carrot.

但引导词 who, what, which, when, where, how 不可省略。有时 that 也不能省略：

I didn't know that the rabbit ate the carrot and that you saw it. (我不知道：1.兔子吃了，2.你看见了)

**(2) 否定前移**：

某些词后的宾语从句的否定：

I think (that) the rabbit is smart. 否定为：I don't think (that) the rabbit is smart. (而不能说 I think that the rabbit isn't smart.)

**(3) 主从时态一致**：

1. I don't think (that) you're right. ➔ 过去式：I didn't think (that) you were right.
2. I knew (that) the rabbit liked carrots. (主句过去，从句过去)
3. 不变的客观现象才是例外：I knew (that) the sun rises in the east.

**(4) be + adj. + that... [宾语从句]**：

形容词通常表达某种看法/判断，that 引导的从句相当于该看法/判断的宾语：

I am sure/happy/afraid that there's a carrot on the table.

#### 3. 表语从句

用一个从句充当表语。主语从句和表语从句二者要强调的信息不同：

系动词为非 be 动词时：

It seems that the rabbit is hungry. (It为形式主语，that从句为表语从句)

It feels that you're not telling the truth.

#### 4. 同位语从句

**同位语**：用不同的方式把一个概念再说一遍，用逗号隔开。如：My teacher, Papa Rabbit, likes carrots. (Papa Rabbit 是 teacher 的同位语)

**同位语从句**：一个从句来充当同位语，常修饰抽象的名词（如 fact, question, idea）。

1. 包含已知信息：The fact that the rabbit ate a carrot did not surprise me. (主语)
2. 包含未知信息：The question whether the rabbit will eat the carrot is on all our mind. (主语，兔子是否会吃胡萝卜这个问题萦绕在我们所有人心中。)
   I have no idea who ate the carrot. (宾语，我不知道谁吃了那胡萝卜)

#### 5. 宾语补语从句

You can call me what you like. (从句充当宾语补语)

### 三、状语从句

#### 1. 时间状语从句

1. 在某一时间点之前 (before)：
   The rabbit ate a carrot at home before the wolf stopped by.

2. 在某一时间点之中/当时 (when, while, as)：
   when = 时间点，突然：The rabbit was eating a carrot when I reached home. (我到家时，兔子正在吃)
   while = 时间段：The rabbit was eating a carrot while I was making a video.
   as = 同时：I ate a carrot as I made a video.

3. 在某一时间点之后 (after)：
   The rabbit ate a carrot after the wolf stopped by.

4. 从之前某一时间前开始算 (since，暗示对之后有影响)：
   The rabbit has shared 3 carrots since the wolf stopped by. (自从狼来串门，兔子已经分享3个了)
   The rabbit has not stopped sharing carrots since the wolf stopped by.

5. 直到之后的某一时间点 (until)：
   The rabbit waited until the wolf stopped by. (兔子一直在等，直到狼来串门)
   The rabbit didn't eat any carrot until the wolf stopped by.

6. 一…就… (as soon as)：
   The rabbit will eat a carrot as soon as the wolf leaves.

7. 下次… (the next time)：
   The rabbit will share the biggest carrot the next time the wolf stops by.

#### 2. 地点状语从句

where 及强调形式：wherever, everywhere, anywhere

**地点状语从句 vs 定语从句**：

状语从句：The rabbit ate the carrot where he found it. (强调在发现它的那个地方)

定语从句：The rabbit ate the carrot at the place where he found it. (修饰先行词 place)

强调不管哪里：

The rabbit will go where / wherever he can find a carrot.

#### 3. 比较状语从句

**比较副词从句："更..." (than)**：

He is smarter than me. / He is smarter than I am.
This carrot is bigger than that one. / This carrot is bigger than that one is.

**比较副词从句："同样地..." (as...as)**：

He is as smart as I am.
This carrot is as big as that one is.

**比较副词从句："越...越..." (the more...the more)**：

The more you practice English, the more fluent you become. (你越多练习英语，你就越流利)
The more coins you give me, the more videos I make.

#### 4. 条件状语从句

"主将从现"：主句将来时，从句一般现在时。一般现在时态强调一般状态、习惯、客观事实；英语的将来时态是指非常确定会发生的事。条件句总是比主句在时态上落后一个时间段（也可应用于虚拟语气，如 If I saw a carrot, I would eat it. 表达与现实相反的条件）。

if：If you like this video, you will give it a thumbs-up.

unless (除非)：Unless you like this video, you will not give it a thumbs-up. = If you don't like this video...

其他引导词：provided (that), as long as, in case

Provided that my video is excellent, you will give it a thumbs-up.

As long as I make excellent videos, you will remain my follower. (只要我一直做高质量视频，你就会继续关注我)

In case you don't understand, please comment below. (如果你不明白，请在下方评论)

#### 5. 让步状语从句

阐述某种情况/做出假设，进一步做出相反/相对的阐述（即使…也… / 尽管…）。

让步假设 (even if，即使没看见)：Even if I see a carrot, I will not eat it.

让步事实 (although/even though，尽管看见了)：Although I see a carrot, I will not eat it.

不管如何让步 (no matter + 疑问词 = regardless of)。

#### 6. 方式状语从句

1. 好像某事发生过的方式；2. 好像某事正在发生的方式 (as if / as though)。

You spend (so much) money, as if you were a millionaire. (就好像你是百万富翁，但你并不是，所以用虚拟语气)

#### 7. 原因状语从句

because, since, as 三者区别（从因果/语气强弱看）：because > since > as

because：引出实实在在的因果关系。because 引导的从句在主句前后都可以。

since：引出大家都已经知道的情况，表示某种原因，"既然"。since 引导的从句一般在主句前。

as：引出的是显而易见的事实，"因为"。as 引导的从句一般也在主句前。

> **注意**：for 也可以表示原因，但 for 构成的是并列复合句，前后两个句子有同等地位，前者是推测，后者是推测的原因。

You must really like me, for you have given a thumb-up.

#### 8. 目的状语从句

有一定的因果关系，使用"目的"的方式说出来。目的状语从句中一般使用情态助词 (can, could, may, might, should, would 等)，更好体现目的性。

so that (不能放句首) / in order that

In order that I could finish the video in time, I pulled an all-nighter.

I pulled an all-nighter so that I could finish the video in time. (我熬夜不睡，就为了及时完成视频)

#### 9. 结果状语从句

主句动作产生的结果，"导致"、"结果是"。

so that：I ate a lot of carrots for lunch, so that I wasn't hungry at all in the afternoon.

so (连接并列句)：I like this video, so I'll give it a thumbs-up.

**so...that... / such...that...** (如此...以至于...)

I ate so many carrots for lunch that I felt sick afterwards.

It was such a tasty carrot that I ate it all at once. (太好吃了，一下子吃完了)

It was so tasty a carrot that I ate it all at once.

结构：so + adj. + that... 或 such + (a/an) + adj. + n. + that...

## 代词

### 一、 人称代词

**主格**：作主语
**宾格**：作宾语

### 二、 物主代词

**形容词性物主代词**：相当于形容词，后面必须加名词。

**名词性物主代词**：相当于名词，后面不再加名词。

例：The carrot is mine.

### 三、 反身代词 / 强调代词

#### 1. 反身代词：作宾语，表示动作回到主语本身。

例：The rabbit washed itself. (兔子把自己洗白白。wash的宾语)

#### 2. 强调代词：作同位语或状语，起强调作用。

例：The rabbit himself washed the carrot. / The rabbit washed the carrot himself. (兔子自己洗了胡萝卜，作状语，起强调作用)

### 四、 相互代词

**each other**：两者之间
**one another**：三者/三者以上之间

例：We should help each other.

### 五、 指示代词

**this / these**：距离说话人较近。后面接名词限定范围，如 this rabbit / these rabbits。
**that / those**：距离说话人较远。如 that rabbit / those rabbits。

### 六、 数量不定代词

#### 1. many / much

many：加可数名词复数（如：many rabbits，许多兔子）
much：加不可数名词（如：much water，许多水）

#### 2. few / little

a few / a little：表肯定，"一些"（a few 加可数名词复数；a little 加不可数名词）
few / little：表否定，"几乎没有"（few 加可数名词复数；little 加不可数名词）

#### 3. some / any

some：用于肯定句。表"一些"。
any：用于否定句/疑问句。表"任何一个"。

特别用法：在期望得到对方正面、肯定回答的疑问句中用 some。

例：Would you like some coffee?

#### 4. each / every

each：可作代词单独作主语；也可以 each + 名词。
every：只能 every + 名词，不可单独作主语。

例：There are many carrots on each side of the street.

#### 5. either / neither (两者)

either：两者中每一个 / 任何一个（谓语动词用单数）。
例：Either the rabbit or that carrot. / Either is good. (两者中任何一个都好)

neither：两者都不 / 每个都不（谓语动词用单数）。
例：Don't give the rabbit this or that carrot. Neither is good. (两者都不好)

#### 6. both / all

both：两者都（谓语动词用复数形式）。
例：Both of the two rabbits are cute.

all：三者/三者以上都（谓语动词用复数形式）。
例：All of the rabbits are cute.

#### 7. other / the other / another

other：另外的，其他的（+名词）。
例：I don't like other carrots.

the other：特指两者中的另一个。
例：This carrot is bigger than the other carrot.

another：3个/3个以上的另一个。
例：Give him another carrot.

#### 8. one / it

one：泛指上文提及的同类事物中的一个（不是数词）。
it：特指上文提及的那个事物本身。

### 七、 复合不定代词

由 some-, any-, no-, every- 加上 -thing, -body, -one 构成。

指人：someone/somebody, anyone/anybody, no one/nobody, everyone/everybody
指物：something, anything, nothing, everything

> **重要规则**：形容词修饰复合不定代词时，必须后置。

例：The rabbit has something / nothing important to do.
例：Nothing can stop the rabbit from eating carrots.

### 八、 疑问代词

包括：who, whom, whose, which, what 等。

作主语（主格）：Who saw the rabbit?
作宾语（宾格）：Whom did the rabbit see?

> **注意**：当疑问代词作介词的宾语且紧跟介词时，必须用宾格 whom。日常中可用 who 代替 whom，但并非所有情况皆可。

### 九、 连接代词

包括：who, whom, whose, what, which, 以及加上 -ever 的 whoever, whomever, whatever, whichever。

**作用**：引导主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句。

加上 -ever：表示"任何"，"一切"，起强调作用。

例：I didn't know who you are. (引导宾语从句)
例：What he said is not true. (引导主语从句)
例：The rabbit wants to eat whatever he likes. (兔子想吃任何他喜欢的东西)

### 十、 关系代词

专用于定语从句。

包括：who, whom, whose, which, that 等。（详请翻看定语从句笔记）

## 形容词

### 一、 形容词的用法

#### 1. 定语

**(1) 前置定语**

修饰名词，放在名词前面。

例：a hungry rabbit (一只饥饿的兔子)

**前置定语顺序**：

限定词(冠词、物主代词) + 主观特征 + 尺寸 + 形状 + 新旧 + 颜色 + 来源 + 材料 + 用途

口诀：美小圆旧黄，中国本书房

例：a beautiful big round new black Chinese modern table (一张美丽的、大的、圆的、新的、黑色的、中国制造的、现代的桌子)

**(2) 后置定语**

修饰不定代词时（something, anything, nothing, somebody, anybody等），形容词必须后置。

somebody nice (某个好人)
everything possible (所有可能的事)
nothing wrong (没错的事)
anybody absent (不在场的任何人)

#### 2. 表语

放在系动词之后，表达主语的性质或状态。

例：The rabbit is hungry.

> **注意**：某些形容词一般作表语，不作定语（如：alive, asleep, alone, ill, well等）。如果需要作定语，需换成对应的形容词：

The rabbit is alive/asleep/alone. ➔ a living/sleeping/lonely rabbit
The rabbit is ill/well. ➔ a sick/healthy rabbit

#### 3. 补语

补充说明宾语的状态，作宾语补语。

例：Carrots make rabbits happy. (胡萝卜让兔子开心。补充说明宾语 rabbit 的状态)

#### 4. 状语

表示主语的状态。

例：Hungry and tired, the rabbit went home. (又饿又累，兔子回家了。)

### 二、 形容词短语作定语

形容词短语作定语时，通常放在被修饰的名词之后（后置）。

介词短语：carrots good for the rabbit (对兔子有益的胡萝卜)

不定式：the rabbit eager to eat carrots = the rabbit that is eager to eat carrots (渴望吃胡萝卜的兔子)

动名词/现在分词：the rabbit busy eating carrots = the rabbit that is busy eating carrots (忙着吃胡萝卜的兔子)

### 三、 复合形容词

#### 1. 形容词 + 形容词/现在分词/过去分词

#### 2. 副词 + 形容词/现在分词/过去分词

#### 3. 名词 + 形容词/现在分词/过去分词

**其他常见复合形容词结构**：

形容词 + 名词 + ed：kind-hearted (好心的) = a woman whose heart is kind
名词 + 名词 + ed：long-distance (长途的)
数词 + 名词 + ed：one-way (单行的)

### 四、 形容词的比较级和最高级

#### 1. 构成形式

#### 2. 用法

**1. A 比 B 更… (比较级 + than)**

This carrot is bigger than that one. (这根胡萝卜比那根大)

倍数修饰：大三倍 ➔ This carrot is three times bigger than that one.
程度修饰：大一点 ➔ a little bigger

**2. A 和 B 一样… (as + 原级 + as)**

This carrot is as big as that one.

倍数修饰：是…的三倍大 ➔ This carrot is three times as big as that one.

**3. 越来越… (比较级 + and + 比较级)**

prettier and prettier = more and more beautiful (越来越漂亮)

**4. 越…越… (the + 比较级, the + 比较级)**

The more carrots the rabbit eats, the smarter he will become. (假设：兔子吃得越多，就会越聪明)
The more carrots the rabbit ate, the smarter he became. (过去：兔子吃得越多，就变得越聪明)
The more, the better. (越多越好)

**5. 最高级 (the + 最高级 + in/of 所属范围)**

Snow White is the most beautiful woman in the world. (白雪公主是世界上最美的女人)

**6. 序数词修饰最高级**

表示"第几最…"。

Who is the second fairest one of all? (谁是世上第二美的人？)

**7. a most 表示"很，非常"**

most 前加 the 表最高级，加 a/an 表"很，非常"。

The queen is a most beautiful woman. (王后是一位非常美丽的女人)

## 副词

### 一、副词的用法

#### 1. 副词修饰动词

例：This rabbit runs fast. (副词修饰动词 runs)

#### 2. 副词修饰形容词

例：This is a very fast rabbit. / This carrot is very good.

#### 3. 副词修饰副词

例：This rabbit runs very fast. / I'm doing very well.

#### 4. 副词修饰名词

例：This rabbit here runs very fast. (副词 here 修饰名词 rabbit)

#### 5. 副词修饰整个句子

例：Obviously, this rabbit here runs very fast. (显然，这里的这只兔子跑得非常快)

> **总结**：副词可以修饰动词、形容词、副词、名词以及整个句子。

### 二、副词在句中充当的成分

#### 1. 状语

例：This is a very fast rabbit. (修饰形容词，作状语)
例：Obviously, this rabbit here runs very fast. (修饰句子和动词，作状语)

#### 2. 表语

例：The wolf is abroad. (狼在国外)

> **注意**：这样做表语的副词（如 abroad, here, there 等）只能用在系动词 be 之后。

#### 3. 宾语补语

例：Let me in. (让我进来) / Let me out. (让我出去)

### 三、形容词与副词的转换

#### 1. 一般直接加 -ly

possible ➔ possibly

#### 2. 以辅音字母 + e 结尾，去 e 加 -y

true ➔ truly

#### 3. 形容词与副词同形

fast：This is a fast rabbit (adj.) ➔ This rabbit runs fast (adv.)
hard：This is a hard carrot (adj.) ➔ I work hard (adv.)

> _(注：hardly 意为"几乎不"，与 hard 意思不同)_

#### 4. 既是形容词也是副词

pretty：
作形容词：This is a pretty wolf. (漂亮的狼)
作副词（相当于 fairly，十分/相当）：This is a fairly pretty wolf. / This is a pretty smart rabbit. (相当聪明的兔子)

#### 5. 形容词 + ly 变副词

quick ➔ quickly：This is a quick rabbit. ➔ This rabbit runs quickly.
large ➔ largely：This is a large carrot. ➔ I largely eat carrots. (我主要吃胡萝卜)

### 四、副词的种类

#### 1. 方式副词

描述动作发生的方式。

例：This rabbit runs fast. / Suddenly, the driver turned left.

位置：可位于句首、句中或句尾。但不是所有副词都可以随意放置。

句中：The driver suddenly turned left.

#### 2. 程度副词

描述动作或特征的程度，一般位于所修饰的词之前。

例：This is a very fast rabbit. (描述了多快，即快的程度)
例：This rabbit runs very fast.

#### 3. 地点副词

表示动作发生的地点，一般位于句尾，但也可位于句首表强调。

indoors / outdoors (室内 / 室外)：The rabbit eats carrots indoors/outdoors.
upstairs / downstairs (楼上 / 楼下)：The rabbit eats carrots upstairs/downstairs.
here / there：Here you can relax and forget about all your troubles. (在这里你可以放松并忘记所有烦恼)

#### 4. 时间副词

表示动作发生的时间，一般位于句尾，也可位于句首。

yesterday：The rabbit ate some carrots yesterday.
now：The rabbit is eating some carrots now.
already：The rabbit has already eaten some carrots.
later：The rabbit will eat some carrots later.

#### 5. 频率副词

表示动作发生的频率（每天、每月、每年、一天两次等）。

例：The rabbit eats carrots every day. / sometimes / usually / three times a day

位置：位于行为动词之前，be动词/助动词/情态动词之后。

The rabbit usually eats carrots.
The rabbit is always hungry.

#### 6. 疑问副词

用于对句子进行提问。

How does the rabbit eat carrots? (方式)
Where does the rabbit eat carrots? (地点)
When does the rabbit eat carrots? (时间)
Why does the rabbit eat carrots? (原因)

#### 7. 连接副词

用于引导名词性从句（主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句）。

宾语从句：I don't know where the rabbit eats carrots.
表语从句：That is why it eats carrots.

#### 8. 关系副词

专用于引导定语从句。

where：This was the place where the rabbit ate the carrot.
why：This is the reason why the rabbit ate the carrot.
when：That was the day when the rabbit ate the carrot for the first time.

#### 9. 句子副词

修饰整个句子，表达说话人的态度或观点，一般位于句子开头，用逗号与主句分开。

例：Obviously, this rabbit here runs very fast. (显然……)

其他常见的句子副词：luckily, fortunately, surprisingly 等。

## 连词

连接有着并列的、同等语法地位的"单词、词组、句子"。

### 一、 并列连词

#### 1. 并列关系

**not only... but also...** (不仅…而且…)

The rabbit not only teaches English but also makes videos.

**neither... nor...** (既不…也不…)

就近原则：Neither the rabbit nor the wolves like apples. (谓语动词用 like，与邻近的主语 the wolves 保持一致)

**either... or...** (要么…要么…)

What does the rabbit like? Apples or carrots?

#### 2. 转折关系

**but** (但是)

**yet** (然而)

The rabbit cooked some carrots, but/yet he didn't eat any. (兔子煮了一些胡萝卜，但他一个也没吃)

**however / nevertheless** (然而，不过)

也可以表否定转折。

#### 3. 因果关系

**so** (所以)

The rabbit is busy, so he won't go to the party tonight.

**for** (因为)

The rabbit will skip lunch, for he is busy making a video. (for 构成并列句，表推测的原因)

**therefore** (因此)

The rabbit is busy, so/therefore he won't go to the party tonight.

**or else / otherwise** (否则)

Hurry up, or else/otherwise you'll be late.

### 二、 从属连词

#### 1. 引导名词性从句

**that**：只起连接作用，在句中没有实际意义。在宾语从句中可以省略，在主语从句、表语从句、同位语从句中不可省略。

宾语从句：I saw (that) the rabbit ate.
主语从句：That the rabbit ate the carrot was obvious.
表语从句：The fact is that the rabbit ate the carrot.

**if / whether** (是否)

宾语从句：I don't know if the rabbit ate the carrot.
同位语从句：The question whether the rabbit will eat the carrot is on all our mind. (只能用 whether 不能用 if)

## 倒装句与被动态

### 一、 倒装句

#### 1. 完全倒装句

将整个谓语动词提前到主语之前。

**1. 副词/介词短语在句首的倒装**

地点副词在句首 (here, there...)：强调动作发生。
There goes the last bus. (末班车开走了。) ➔ 正常语序：The last bus goes there.

时间副词在句首：
Now comes the wolf's turn. (现在轮到狼了。) ➔ 正常语序：The wolf's turn comes now.

表运动方向的副词在句首 (in, out, up, down, away...)：使动作显得更加生动，容易有画面感。
Up went the carrots into the air. (胡萝卜飞到了空中。) ➔ 正常语序：Carrots went up into the air.

介词短语在句首：
主系表结构对主语进行补充说明时的倒装。

**2. 主语补语(表语)的倒装**

作表语的形容词、分词、介词短语、such 置于句首时，引起完全倒装。

#### 2. 部分倒装句

分离助动词和谓语动词，仅将助动词提前。

**1. 句首有否定意义词时的倒装**

Never before have I eaten such a delicious carrot. (我以前从未吃过这么好吃的胡萝卜。) ➔ 正常语序：I have never before eaten such a delicious carrot.

**2. 句首有 "only" 时的倒装**

Only in this way can we grow delicious carrots. (只有用这种方法我们才能种出美味的胡萝卜。)
Only then did the rabbit meet the wolf. (只有在那时兔子才遇到了狼。)

**3. "if...should..." 构成虚拟语气的倒装**

去掉 if，将 should 提前。

正常虚拟语序：If I should win the lottery, I would buy a huge pile of carrots.
倒装：Should I win the lottery, I would buy a huge pile of carrots. (如果我中了大奖，我会买一大堆胡萝卜。)

**4. 固定句型中的倒装**

So + 助动词/系动词/情态动词 + 主语 (表示"也一样")：
Rabbits love eating carrots; So do wolves. (兔子爱吃胡萝卜，狼也是。)
—I was afraid. —So was I. (我也害怕。)

neither / nor + 助动词/系动词/情态动词 + 主语 (表示"也不")：
The rabbit can't ride a bicycle; neither/nor can the wolf.

#### 3. 形式倒装句

只把强调的内容提至句首，谓语动词并不提前。

**1. 感叹句中的倒装**

What a delicious carrot it is!

**2. 比较级句型中的倒装 (如 "the more..., the more...")**

The more carrots you eat, the healthier you become. (你吃越多胡萝卜，就越健康。) = If you eat more carrots, you will become healthier.

**3. "however", "whatever" 引导让步状语从句时的倒装**

However long this video is, you should watch it till the end. (无论这个视频有多长，你都应该看到最后。)

**4. "as", "though" 引导让步状语从句时的倒装**

将形容词/副词/名词等提至句首。

Much as he likes the carrot, he doesn't want to eat it. (尽管他很喜欢胡萝卜，但他现在不想吃。)

### 二、 被动语态

基本结构：be + 过去分词 + by + 动作执行者

核心作用：强调动作的承受者。

**基本句型与被动语态的转换**

**特别注意：使役动词 make 的被动语态**

主动句中 make 后接省略 to 的不定式作宾补；改为被动语态时，必须把 to 还原。

主动：The rabbit made the wolf laugh. (兔子把狼逗笑了。)
被动：The wolf was made to laugh by the rabbit.

## 主谓一致

### 概述

**汉语（孤立语）**：词形几乎无变化，动词不存在语法意义上的变位。

**英语（屈折语）**：以词形变化表达语法意义，谓语动词需携带人称、单复数表时态，语序相对自由。

**主谓一致**：主语和谓语传递的信息是一致的，即主谓在数上保持一致。主要遵循三大原则：

- 语法一致
- 意义一致
- 就近一致

### 一、 语法一致

谓语动词在形式上与主语保持单复数一致。

#### 1. 单数主语 / 复数主语

不可数名词作主语：看作单数，谓语用单数。
Time is money. (time 不可数)

可数名词复数作主语：谓语用复数。
Carrots are delicious.
Carrots have been eaten.

不可数名词 + 谓语动词单数：
Carrot juice has been sold.

#### 2. 非谓语动词作主语

不定式或动名词作主语，看作单数名词，谓语用单数。

To eat a carrot every day is good for the rabbit.
Eating carrots is healthy.

#### 3. and 连接的主语

用 and 连接两个主语，通常表示复数概念，用复数谓语动词。（但不是所有 and 都用复数，详见"意义一致"）

The rabbit and wolf are at the party.

#### 4. 假象主语（就远原则）

当主语后跟以下词组补充说明时，谓语动词与前面的核心主语保持一致，不受后面补充信息的影响：

as well as, except, but

The rabbit as well as other animals gets a carrot. (核心主语是 rabbit，单数)
All animals except the wolf get the carrots. (核心主语是 animals，复数)

#### 5. 不定代词作主语

谓语动词和不定代词背后的概念保持一致。

表达"每一个"：指单数，用单数谓语动词。
Everyone gets a carrot.
Each of them gets a carrot.

表达"他们/两个"：指复数，用复数谓语动词。
Both of them like carrots.
Some of them are afraid of the wolf.

表达"没有"：单复数谓语动词均可。
None of them get / gets a carrot.
Neither of them is / are afraid of the wolf.

### 二、 意义一致

主语意义为单数，谓语动词用单数；主语意义为复数，谓语动词用复数。

#### 1. 同一主语多种身份

同一人多个身份：主语在意义上是一个人，用单数。
The teacher and video creator is a rabbit. (一个人：既是老师又是视频创作者)

不同人：主语的意义变成2个人，用复数。
The teacher and the video creator are rabbits. (两个人：老师和视频创作者)

#### 2. 复数名词的单数意义

形式上是复数，但表达的意义是单数（作为一个整体）。

表示时间段/金额：
Ten years is a long time. (十年是一段长时间)
Two coins is not a lot. (两枚硬币不算多)

配套的物品（表示一副/一套）：
A knife and fork is needed for eating the carrot. (表示一副刀叉，用单数)
Bread and butter is also needed at dinner. (黄油面包算作一份食物，用 is，用 are 也不算错)

#### 3. 不定代词的单复数形式

视具体语义而判断。

指抽象的"一切"：用单数。
All is quiet.
Everything is quiet.

指"很多人/大家"：结合上下文，用复数。
All are quiet.

#### 4. 名词的形和意

**(1) 形复意单（用单数谓语动词）**

Physics is my favorite subject. (物理学科)

**(2) 形单意复（用复数谓语动词）**

The cattle are on the hill. (牛群在山上)

**(3) 单复数同形名词（单/复数谓语动词皆可，看具体意义）**

A sheep is eating grass.
The sheep are eating grass.

类似名词：deer, means, series, species

**(4) 集合名词（不是盲选，看具体意义）**

1. 既可表集合（单数意义），又可表集合中的全部成员（复数意义）

   The rabbit's family is huge. (强调"这个集合/家庭"，用单数)
   The rabbit's family are all at home. (强调"所有人/家庭成员"，用复数)

   类似名词：army, audience, police, team 等。

2. 只能表示一个类别的事物，只能用单数谓语动词

   All the furniture here is expensive. (家具永远不可数！)

3. the + 形容词 可视作集合名词，表单/复数意义

   The young are expected to learn from the old. (表示"年轻的人们"，复数)
   The new is going to replace the old. (表示"新事物"这一抽象概念，单数)

### 三、 就近一致（原则）

谓语动词单复数由最近的主语决定。

#### 1. 特定连词连接的主语

由 or, either...or..., neither...nor..., not only...but also... 连接两个名词或代词作主语时，谓语动词由离它最近的主语保持一致。

Either you or I am going to the party.
Not only the rabbit but also many other animals like it.

#### 2. There be 句型

There is a carrot and two apples on the table.
There are two apples and a carrot on the table.

### 四、 补充规则

#### 1. 谓语动词用单数的情况

1. 以 -ics 结尾的学科名称：politics, physics, mathematics, statistics 等。

2. 表示金钱、时间、价格或度量衡的名词作主语时，常视作整体：
   Ten miles seems like a long walk.

3. 以 -sis 结尾表示疾病的词：arthritis (关节炎), diabetes (糖尿病), phlebitis (静脉炎), mumps (腮腺炎) 等。

#### 2. 谓语动词用复数的情况

1. 群岛：the West Indies (西印度群岛)

2. 山脉：the Himalayas (喜马拉雅山脉)

3. 海峡、瀑布
